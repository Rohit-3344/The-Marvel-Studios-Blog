Welcome To The Marvel Studios Blog

Marvel Studios was founded in 1993 as Marvel Films and later rebranded as Marvel Studios in 1996. It became a major player in the entertainment industry with the creation of the MCU, which began in 2008 with the release of Iron Man. Since then, the MCU has grown into one of the most successful film franchises in history, producing blockbusters, TV shows, and spinoffs, all within a shared universe.
